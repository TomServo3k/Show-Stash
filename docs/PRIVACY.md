# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Show Stash is provided by Pearl Lane, LLC.

This policy describes how Show Stash handles information when you use the Roku channel.

## Information Show Stash Stores

Show Stash stores the following information on your Roku device:

- Your household code.
- Your saved show list.
- App settings, including your TMDb API key if you enter one.
- Whether Hulu and ESPN matches should be saved as Disney+.

## Information Show Stash Sends To Services

Show Stash may send show search text to TMDb when TMDb lookup is enabled. TMDb returns show metadata and provider information used to help you add shows.

Show Stash syncs your household show list through Firebase Realtime Database using your household code. This lets multiple Roku devices in the same household share the same list.

Show Stash launches or opens Roku Channel Store pages for selected streaming services. Those third-party services are governed by their own privacy policies.

## Information Show Stash Does Not Collect

Show Stash does not require a Pearl Lane account. Show Stash does not collect names, email addresses, payment information, precise location, voice recordings, photos, or videos.

## Children

Show Stash is intended for household organization and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Third-Party Services

Show Stash uses or may interact with:

- TMDb for show metadata and provider lookup.
- Firebase Realtime Database for household list sync.
- Roku and installed Roku channels for channel launching.

Review the privacy policies for those services to understand their data practices.
